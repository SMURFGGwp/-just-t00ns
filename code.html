FAQ

What are just t00ns?

expand_more

Just t00ns are a collection of 5,555 unique clay-style characters living on the blockchain. They represent the spirit of creative chaos and internet magic.

How can I get one?

expand_more

You can earn your spot through art contests on Discord, active community participation, or by participating in the public minting event.
